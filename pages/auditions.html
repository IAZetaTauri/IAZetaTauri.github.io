Auditions

We are pleased to announce that open auditions have begun, as of June 12, 2023 and will continue until February 24, 2024. As this is planned to be the first girl group to debut under HELIX :: ri Entertainment, this round of open auditions will give preference to persons between the age of 18 to 26.

We strongly believe creativity and talent can come from anywhere, and from any person. We would like to encourage those who wish to apply to do so.

Rules & Limitations

  • Preference will be given to persons between the ages of 18 to 26
  • Must be able to travel to Manhattan, New York, USA
  • Must be able to travel to Seoul, South Korea
  • Must be able to relocate to Seoul, South Korea
  • Must be able to Live in Seoul, South Korea for an extended period of time
  • All creative work must be original

General Information

Subsequent trainees will be based in Seoul, South Korea, and will be managed by HELIX :: RI Entertainment Korea, under Taeyeon Song. Auditions will be held in three rounds; the first being a digital audition, while the last two will be held in person. Should a participant advance to round two of auditions, they need to be able to travel, as auditions will thereafter be held in person. We are able to offer accommodations to participants who may need it, in the form of reduced costs and fee waivers, as well as paid travel. E-mails will be sent to those who advance to the next rounds of auditions with further information. If no contact is made in two weeks, consider it a dropout.

Locations of Physical Auditions

  • Manhattan, New York, USA | HELIX :: ri Building @ 2:00pm EDT December 12, 2023
  • Seoul, South Korea | HELIX :: ri Building @ 1:30pm KST January 25, 2024
