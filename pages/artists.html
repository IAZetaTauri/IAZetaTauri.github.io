Sugar HI

Sugar HI debuted in March of 2023. New to the scene, they've made waves with their debut album, Dream :: START, a bright and catchy pop album.

Propaganda

Propaganda debuted in September of 2020. Their emphasis on heavy rock combined with the lyrical poetry in their hard hitting rap verses have ensured them a place in the industry. Already winning many awards, they show no sign of slowing down.

StrayE

StrayE debuted in March of 2018. Not quite veterans, and not quite rookies, they've continued to push the boundaries of music. Not afraid to try new genres and sounds, this self produced group has gone on to win many awards.

PDR

Perfect Dream Requiem (PDR) debuted in April 2013. Veterans in the industry, their songs span many genres, usually mixing flowing raps with dream like vocals. They've won many awards, and have shattered records across the globe, winning the hearts of fans internationally.
