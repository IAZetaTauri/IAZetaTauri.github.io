Who We Are

Founded in April, 2008, our Flagship Headquarters are located in Manhattan, New York. Through the years, our vision has remained constant; connecting people through music. With a deep dedication to this ideal as well as to our community, we've poured our time and effort into funding music schools as well as aspiring artists. We're able to do this through our musical scholarship program, volunteers, investors, and those who support our artists. Ready to bring our passion internationally, we've recently announced the opening of our South Korean Headquarters, located in Seoul.
