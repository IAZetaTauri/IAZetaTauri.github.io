Who We Are

With Flagship Headquarters in Manhattan, New York, we take pride in our ties to the local music scene. Founding in April of 2008, our vision has remained constant; connecting people through music. When it comes to music, the possibilities are limitless. With a deep dedication to music and our local music scene, we've poured our time and efforts into funding music schools as well as aspiring artists. We're able to do this through our musical scholarship program, volunteers, investors, and those who support our artists. Ready to bring our passion internationally, we've recently began construction on our South Korean Headquarters, located in Seoul.
