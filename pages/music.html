4th Full Length Album

STRAYE — RE–play

Genre

Hip–Hop, R&B, Pop

Length

23 Minutes

Concept

Mixing old school Hip–hop with modern R&B and Pop, STRAYE's producer unit wanted to bring a fresh but familiar sound with this album. A fun play on this concept, STRAYE members decided on the name RE–play for their 3rd Full Length Album. STRAYE also helped to design the cover of the album, using nostalgic elements such as cassettes, pixel text, and vintage stickers. The album also features unreleased mix–tapes created by sub–units in the group, which were made pre–debut. This gives more meaning to the name RE–play, giving the members a chance to re–live past memories with their fans.

2nd Full Length Album

Para:digm — Sector 1 : MOVEMENT

Genre

Indie Rock, Alternative Rock, Hard Rock

Length

32 Minutes

Concept

Para:digm wanted to produce a heavy and rebellious sound with this album, utilizing heavy guitar riffs and booming vocals to get their point across. It's the very idea of rebellion that gave inspiration for the name of their 2nd Album, Sector 1 : MOVEMENT. The album cover was designed by one of Para:digm's own: San. He created the design with input from the members, describing the cover as follows, "The red represents the aggression we can feel when we're stimulated to act, while the spray paint font gives an impression of graffiti art, which is often used to spread political and social messages. I wanted to portray the sound of the album visually, before a listener had even heard a single song". When asked why he added the phrase "On Air", San said he wanted to represent the broadcasting of their message.
